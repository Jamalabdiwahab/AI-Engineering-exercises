Artificial Intelligence is transforming education by enabling personalized learning, intelligent tutoring systems, automated grading, and improved administrative efficiency, making education more accessible and engaging. While AI offers significant benefits such as scalability and data-driven insights, it also presents challenges related to data privacy, bias, equity, and overdependence on technology. Looking ahead, the responsible integration of AI—with collaboration among educators, technologists, and policymakers—promises to enhance educational experiences and prepare learners for a digital future.
